What is EDI automation?

The standard exchange of business, automated.

Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) is the standardized electronic exchange of business documents — purchase orders, invoices, advance ship notices, and more — between trading partners. Crstl is an AI-native EDI and supply-chain commerce platform that automates order-to-cash workflows between brands, retailers, and logistics partners. Onboard new retail trading partners in days instead of months, automatically generate compliant ASNs and invoices, and connect seamlessly with Shopify, NetSuite, Microsoft Dynamics 365, QuickBooks, Amazon, Walmart, Target, Costco, and hundreds more.
