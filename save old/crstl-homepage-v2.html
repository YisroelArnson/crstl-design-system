What is EDI automation?

The standard exchange of business, automated.

Electronic Data Interchange moves purchase orders, invoices, advance ship notices, and acknowledgments between trading partners. Crstl turns that required standard into an AI-native order-to-cash layer for brands, retailers, and logistics teams.

Onboard trading partners in days instead of months. Generate compliant ASNs and invoices, resolve exceptions before they become chargebacks, and connect to Shopify, NetSuite, Microsoft Dynamics 365, QuickBooks, Amazon, Walmart, Target, Costco, and more.

FAQ

Your EDI questions, answered.

A quick read on platform fit, onboarding, integrations, chargebacks, and document coverage.

What is Crstl?

Crstl is an AI-powered EDI and B2B commerce platform that automates order-to-cash workflows between brands, retailers, and logistics providers.

How long does onboarding take?

Most brands can move from setup to live transactions in days or weeks, depending on partner requirements, data readiness, and integration scope.

Which trading partners are supported?

Crstl supports major retailers and marketplaces including Walmart, Target, Costco, Amazon, UNFI, Whole Foods, Kroger, and hundreds more.

Does Crstl integrate with my ERP or store?

Yes. Crstl connects with NetSuite, Shopify, Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central, QuickBooks, and other ERP and commerce platforms.

What EDI documents does Crstl support?

Crstl supports standard transaction sets including 850 purchase orders, 855 acknowledgments, 856 ASNs, 810 invoices, 820 payments, 997 acknowledgments, and custom mappings.

Can Crstl help reduce chargebacks?

Yes. Edison validates documents against retailer-specific requirements before submission, catching ASN, label, and routing-guide issues earlier in the workflow.
